# User Interviews

## Interview #1: Procurement Manager at a SaaS company
- **Problem:** Multiple AI subscriptions were purchased by product and marketing teams without centralized oversight.
- **Pain point:** Difficulty tracking recurring spend across ChatGPT, Claude, and niche AI services.
- **Desired outcome:** A simple way to see total monthly AI spend and high-priority savings opportunities.
- **Key insight:** Procurement needs a single audit summary that can be shared with leadership.

## Interview #2: AI Team Lead at a growth-stage startup
- **Problem:** The team had redundant tooling and unclear license overlap.
- **Pain point:** Too many subscriptions and no easy way to know which plans were wasteful.
- **Desired outcome:** Recommendations for plan consolidation and lower-cost alternatives.
- **Key insight:** AI spend optimization is more valuable when paired with vendor consolidation suggestions.

## Interview #3: Finance Director at a mid-market tech business
- **Problem:** Finance wanted better visibility into AI spend and ROI.
- **Pain point:** Audit reports were manual and inconsistent, with no executive summary.
- **Desired outcome:** Automated cost analysis and actionable recommendations for monthly finance review.
- **Key insight:** Executive summaries and ROI estimates are essential for finance stakeholders to make decisions quickly.
