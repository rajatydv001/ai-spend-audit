# Economics

## Revenue model
- **Free tier:** entry-level access with a limit on audits and basic reporting.
- **Pro tier:** subscription revenue with expanded audit limits, AI insights, and analytics.
- **Enterprise tier:** custom pricing for organizations with advanced collaboration and support needs.

## Pricing assumptions
- Pro plan priced to cover platform costs and generate margin on AI, hosting, and support.
- Enterprise pricing remains custom to support negotiation and higher-value contracts.

## Unit economics
- **Average revenue per account (ARPA):** $200/month for Pro.
- **Gross margin:** 70%+ after cloud hosting, Stripe fees, and AI API costs.
- **Payback period:** 3-4 months if CAC is kept under $600.

## Cost drivers
- OpenAI API usage for premium insights.
- Stripe payment and subscription processing fees.
- Notification and email delivery costs.
- Database and hosting costs for multi-tenant organizations.

## Business KPIs
- **MRR growth** from conversions and upgrades.
- **Customer acquisition cost (CAC)** through marketing and ads.
- **Lifetime value (LTV)** via retention and plan expansion.
- **Churn** from inactive or low-value accounts.
