# Go-To-Market Plan

## Target customer
- Finance and procurement leaders at mid-market technology companies
- AI platform managers and operations teams responsible for subscription spend
- Small to medium enterprises using multiple AI tools and APIs

## Value proposition
AI Spend Audit helps teams uncover wasted AI subscription spend and provides clear optimization recommendations so they can reduce costs and maximize ROI.

## Key messages
- Stop overpaying for AI tools.
- Discover better plans and consolidate overlapping subscriptions.
- Turn AI spend into a measurable cost-optimization initiative.

## Launch channels
- LinkedIn ads targeting AI procurement and finance roles
- Product Hunt launch with AI/fintech communities
- Cold outreach to SaaS founders and AI team leads
- Email campaigns to procurement and finance newsletters

## Activation strategy
- Offer a free audit trial that shows potential savings immediately.
- Capture leads using sign-up and audit report forms.
- Follow up with a conversion email highlighting ROI and plan benefits.

## Growth levers
- Content marketing: publish AI spend audit case studies and savings playbooks.
- Partnerships: integrate with finance tools and procurement platforms.
- Referral incentives: offer discounts or credits for referrals from current users.
