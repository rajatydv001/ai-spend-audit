# Metrics

## Key performance indicators

- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** Track active subscription revenue from Pro and Enterprise customers.
- **Audit Activation Rate:** Percentage of signups that complete their first audit.
- **Upgrade Conversion Rate:** Percentage of users who move from Free to Pro or Enterprise.
- **Retention Rate:** Percentage of paying customers who renew each month.
- **Average Savings per Audit:** Average monthly savings estimated by the audit engine.
- **AI Optimization Score:** Average score across all audits, indicating cost efficiency.
- **Churn Rate:** Percentage of customers who cancel or fail to renew.

## Operational metrics
- **Time to first audit:** Average time from signup to first completed audit.
- **Notification engagement:** Open/click rate for optimization and savings alerts.
- **Lead capture rate:** Percentage of audit visitors who sign up or request pricing.
- **Support response time:** Time to respond to premium and enterprise support requests.

## Reporting cadence
- Weekly dashboard updates for usage and adoption.
- Monthly executive summary for revenue and audit performance.
- Quarterly review of pricing, churn, and customer segment metrics.
